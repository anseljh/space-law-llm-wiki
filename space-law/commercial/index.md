# Commercial Space Activity

Private-sector launch, reentry, resource, remote-sensing, satellite, and spaceport activity and its licensing.

## Concepts

* [Commercial Space Launch & Reentry Licensing](commercial-launch-licensing.md) - FAA/AST licensing of commercial launch and reentry operations under 51 U.S.C. ch. 509 and the streamlined 14 CFR Part 450 framework (and predecessor Parts 400-440).
* [Space Resource Exploration & Utilization (SPACE Act 2015)](space-resources.md) - Commercial rights to recover and use space resources under 51 U.S.C. ch. 513 and EO 13914 on international support for recovery and use of space resources.
* [Liability & Indemnification for Space Activities](liability-indemnification.md) - Government indemnification, financial responsibility, and cross-waivers for launch/reentry (51 U.S.C. ss 50914-50919), framed by the international Liability Convention.
* [Satellite Communications & Spectrum Licensing](satellite-comms-spectrum.md) - Communications satellite policy under the COMSAT Act (47 U.S.C. ch. 6) and FCC space-station / earth-station licensing at 47 CFR Part 25.
* [Commercial Remote Sensing](commercial-remote-sensing.md) - Licensing and policy for private Earth remote-sensing space systems under the Land Remote Sensing Policy Act (51 U.S.C. ch. 601-603).
* [Commercial Space Competitiveness & Recent Executive Action](commercial-competitiveness-recent-action.md) - Commercial space competitiveness provisions (51 U.S.C. ch. 511) and recent executive action enabling competition and U.S. space superiority (EO 14335, 14369) plus 2025 commercial-space rulemakings.
* [Spaceports & Launch Site Operations](spaceports-launch-sites.md) - Licensing and operation of launch and reentry sites (spaceports), including 14 CFR Part 420 (launch site) and Part 433 (reentry site).
* [Other Space-Law Authorities](other-space-authorities.md) - Space-law slice documents not claimed by a more specific topic.
