# International Space Law

Treaties and the international legal framework governing activities in outer space.

## Concepts

* [Registration of Space Objects](registration-of-space-objects.md) - International obligations to register objects launched into outer space under the Registration Convention.
* [Outer Space Treaty & International Framework](outer-space-treaty-framework.md) - Foundational international space law: the Outer Space Treaty's principles (non-appropriation, peaceful use, state responsibility) and U.S. policy implementing them.
