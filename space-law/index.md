# Space Law

An experimental Open Knowledge Format bundle over the `space-law` content-module slice of Nomolith, with cross-references induced from the extracted citation graph (issue #335).

## Concept Groups

* [Commercial Space Activity](commercial/) - Private-sector launch, reentry, resource, remote-sensing, satellite, and spaceport activity and its licensing.
* [International Space Law](international/) - Treaties and the international legal framework governing activities in outer space.
* [Government Space Programs](government/) - Federal civil and national-security space programs and their organic authorities.

* [graph.json](graph.json) - OKF-style citation graph — 851 nodes, 241 edges (intra 4 · outbound 233 · inbound 4)
